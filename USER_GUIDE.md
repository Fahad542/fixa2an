## üë§ For Customers

### How Fixa2an Works for Customers

Fixa2an connects you with verified repair workshops in your area. Here's how it works:

1. **Upload** your vehicle inspection report
2. **Receive** offers from nearby workshops
3. **Compare** prices, ratings, and services
4. **Book** your preferred workshop
5. **Get** your vehicle repaired!

---

### Step 1: Upload Inspection Report

**What you need:**
- Photos or documents of your vehicle inspection report (JPG, PNG, or PDF)
- Vehicle information (make, model, year)
- Your location

**How to upload:**

1. **Sign in** to your account
2. Click on **"Upload"** in the navigation menu or go to the Upload page
3. **Upload files:**
   - Click "Choose Files" or drag and drop your inspection report images/documents
   - Supported formats: JPG, PNG, PDF (max 10MB per file)
   - You can upload multiple files
4. **Enter vehicle information:**
   - **Make**: e.g., Volvo, BMW, Toyota
   - **Model**: e.g., XC60, 3 Series, Corolla
   - **Year**: e.g., 2020
   - **Registration Number** (optional)
5. **Add description** (optional):
   - Describe any specific issues or concerns
6. **Enter your location:**
   - Your address, city, and postal code
   - This helps workshops calculate distance and provide accurate quotes
7. **Click "Submit"**

**What happens next:**
- Your request is created and sent to nearby verified workshops
- Workshops will receive notifications about your request
- You'll be redirected to "My Cases" to track your request

---

### Step 2: View and Compare Offers

**When offers are ready:**

1. Go to **"My Cases"** from the navigation menu
2. Find your request with status **"Offers Ready"**
3. Click **"See Details"** button

**On the Offers page, you can:**

- **View all offers** from different workshops
- **Sort offers** by:
  - **Price** (lowest to highest)
  - **Distance** (nearest first)
  - **Rating** (highest rated first)
- **Filter offers** by:
  - Price range
  - Maximum distance
  - Minimum rating
- **See offer details** including:
  - Workshop name and rating
  - Price (including VAT)
  - Distance from your location
  - Estimated repair duration
  - Warranty information
  - Workshop notes/message

**To view more details:**
- Click **"View Details & Book"** on any offer
- You'll see complete information about the workshop and the offer

---

### Step 3: Book an Appointment

**To book with a workshop:**

1. On the Offers page, click **"View Details & Book"** on your preferred offer
2. **Select a date and time:**
   - Choose from available time slots provided by the workshop
   - Some workshops may offer flexible scheduling
3. **Add booking notes** (optional):
   - Any special instructions or requirements
4. **Review the details:**
   - Confirm the price, workshop, and appointment time
5. **Click "Book Appointment"**

**After booking:**
- You'll receive a confirmation
- The workshop will be notified
- Your booking will appear in "My Cases" with status **"Booked"**
- You can view booking details anytime from "My Cases"

---

### Managing Your Cases

**View all your requests and bookings:**

1. Go to **"My Cases"** from the navigation menu
2. You'll see all your:
   - **Active Requests** (waiting for offers)
   - **Requests with Offers** (ready to book)
   - **Booked Appointments** (upcoming)
   - **Completed Jobs** (finished repairs)
   - **Cancelled** (cancelled requests/bookings)

**Request Statuses:**

- **üü° Waiting for Offers**: Your request is active, workshops are submitting offers
- **üü¢ Offers Ready**: You have offers available to view and compare
- **üîµ Booked**: You've booked an appointment with a workshop
- **‚úÖ Completed**: The repair work has been completed
- **‚ùå Cancelled**: The request or booking was cancelled

**Actions you can take:**

#### View Request Details
- Click **"See Details"** to view all offers for a request
- Compare workshops and prices

#### Cancel a Booking
- Find your booking in "My Cases"
- Click **"Cancel Booking"**
- **Important**: Free cancellation if done 24+ hours before appointment
- Cancellations within 24 hours may have restrictions

#### Reschedule an Appointment
- Find your booking in "My Cases"
- Click **"Reschedule"**
- Select a new date and time
- **Note**: Free rescheduling if done 24+ hours before appointment

#### Leave a Review
- After a completed job, you can leave a review
- Click **"Leave Review"** on completed bookings
- Rate the workshop (1-5 stars)
- Write a review comment
- Your review helps other customers make informed decisions

## üîß For Workshops

### How Fixa2an Works for Workshops

Fixa2an helps you connect with customers who need vehicle repairs. Here's the workflow:

1. **Register** your workshop
2. **Browse** available customer requests
3. **Submit** competitive offers
4. **Get booked** by customers
5. **Complete** the work and get paid

---

### Workshop Registration

**To register as a workshop:**

1. Click **"Sign Up"** on the homepage
2. Select **"Workshop"** account type
3. **Fill in workshop details:**
   - Personal information (name, email, password)
   - **Company information:**
     - Company name
     - Organization number
     - Address and location
     - Phone number
     - Website (optional)
   - **Business details:**
     - Description of services
     - Opening hours (Monday-Sunday)
     - Brands you specialize in
   - **Documents** (if required):
     - Business license
     - Certifications
4. **Submit registration**

**After registration:**
- Your account will be created
- Your workshop profile will be pending admin verification
- Once verified, you'll be able to receive customer requests and submit offers

---

### Workshop Dashboard

**Your dashboard shows:**
- **Total Requests**: Number of customer requests in your area
- **Active Offers**: Offers you've submitted that are still active
- **Completed Jobs**: Successfully completed repair jobs
- **Total Revenue**: Your earnings from completed jobs

---

### Viewing Available Requests

**To see customer requests:**

1. Go to **"Requests"** from the navigation menu
2. You'll see a list of available requests in your area
3. **Each request shows:**
   - Customer location and distance
   - Vehicle information (make, model, year)
   - Inspection report files
   - Customer description
   - Request expiry date

**To view request details:**
- Click on a request card
- View inspection report images
- See customer information
- Check distance and location

---

### Creating an Offer

**To submit an offer:**

1. Find a request you're interested in
2. Click **"Create Offer"** or **"Submit Offer"**
3. **Fill in offer details:**
   - **Price**: Total price including VAT (in SEK)
   - **Estimated Duration**: How long the repair will take
   - **Warranty**: Warranty period offered (if applicable)
   - **Available Dates**: When you can perform the work
   - **Notes**: Any additional information for the customer
4. **Review your offer**
5. **Click "Submit Offer"**

**Important:**
- Offers expire after 48 hours if not accepted
- Customers can see all offers and compare them
- Make your offer competitive but fair
- Be clear about what's included in the price

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### For Customers

**Q: How much does it cost to use Fixa2an?**
A: Creating an account and submitting requests is free. You only pay the workshop directly for the repair work.

**Q: How do I know if a workshop is trustworthy?**
A: All workshops on Fixa2an are verified. You can see their ratings and reviews from other customers before booking.

**Q: Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?**
A: Yes! You can cancel or reschedule free of charge if done 24+ hours before the appointment. Cancellations within 24 hours may have restrictions.

**Q: What if I'm not satisfied with the work?**
A: Contact the workshop directly first. If issues persist, you can leave a review and contact Fixa2an support for assistance.

**Q: How long do I have to wait for offers?**
A: Workshops typically respond within a few hours. Your request is active for 48 hours, during which workshops can submit offers.

**Q: Do I pay through Fixa2an?**
A: Payment is handled directly with the workshop. Fixa2an facilitates the connection but doesn't process payments.

### For Workshops

**Q: How much does it cost to join Fixa2an?**
A: Registration is free. Fixa2an charges a commission on completed jobs (typically around 10%).

**Q: How do I get verified?**
A: After registration, our admin team reviews your workshop information and documents. Once approved, you can start receiving requests.

**Q: How are requests matched to my workshop?**
A: Requests are matched based on:
- Geographic proximity (within your service radius)
- Vehicle brands you specialize in
- Your workshop verification status

**Q: What happens if a customer cancels?**
A: If cancelled 24+ hours before the appointment, there's no penalty. Cancellations within 24 hours are handled on a case-by-case basis.

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Payment is handled directly with the customer. Fixa2an sends monthly invoices for platform commission.

**Q: Can I edit my offer after submitting?**
A: Once submitted, offers cannot be edited. However, you can contact the customer directly if needed, or wait for the offer to expire and submit a new one.
